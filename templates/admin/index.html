Detection guide

Source images that make accurate proposals easier.
  1. Best option: choose a mockup with a clear gray dashed rectangle marking the artwork replacement area.
  2. Otherwise prefer a front-facing frame with a clear inner printable boundary.
  3. Keep text, shadows and decorative objects outside the artwork opening.
  4. Use a separate transparent foreground layer for objects overlapping artwork.
  5. Always review and adjust the detection proposal before approval.
