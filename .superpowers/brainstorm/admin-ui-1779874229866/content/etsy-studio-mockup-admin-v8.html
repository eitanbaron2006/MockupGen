Create detection-friendly mockups

Source image guidelines for reliable AI artwork-area proposals.
  1. Keep one empty insertion area with visible contrast from the frame or mat.
  2. Use a continuous solid or dashed border around the target area, about 2-4 px, without plants, text, reflections, or shadows crossing it.
  3. For simple PNG rendering, keep the artwork area front-facing and rectangular. Angled or perspective frames require a future warp or PSD mode.
  4. Do not place "YOUR ARTWORK HERE" text inside the final replacement region unless it is only a temporary source image.
  5. Create separate square, portrait, and landscape variants for each product category.
